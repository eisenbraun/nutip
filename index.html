NüTip Beta

by Michael Eisenbraun

NüTip is a jQuery plugin that gives you several different styles to provide visitors with additional information.

Tips

Hover over the swatch and the tip will appear above.

Nuggets

Hover over the swatch and the nugget will fly in from the side

Popups

Click on the swatch to reveal the popup.

Notifications

Click on the swatch to trigger the notification.

Growl

Click on the swatch to trigger the growl.
